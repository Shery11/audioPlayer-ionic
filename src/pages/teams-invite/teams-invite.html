How does this work?

An invitation email is sent to the user or users you entered. Once they accept your invitation and create an account, you’ll see them in your dashboard as active.

What happens if they don’t receive the email

First you should check that the user was added correctly to your group, if you entered the wrong email address, you can simply delete the user added and re-invite them following the same proccess.

If the email has been entered correctly, simply visit thier detail page and hit the re-send invite button. Make sure you tell the member of your group to check the spam box as this may be the case why they can’t see the invite.
